    Find Us

    A website designer, or what is known in English (Web designer), is a person who specializes in building information technology websites, and is responsible for designing the visual appearance of the website and its ease of use. He uses various design tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and others to help create the design

    1- Good knowledge of programming languages ​​and various technologies used in web development such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and others.
    2- Graphic interface,user experience and content design skills.
    3- The ability to communicate well with the team and work independently.
    4- The ability to manage projects and determine tasks and priorities

    A web designer creates and oversees all visual aspects of websites. Web designers plan, invent and create websites and web pages, many of which combine a number of visual elements including text, images, graphics and videos.
